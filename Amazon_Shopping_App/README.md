1. Identified problem statement by doing user research for Amazon shopping app.
2. Used AI tools to conduct research, document PRD and create prototype.

Problem statement:
- Product discovery is slow & time consuming as search does not return the most relevant results for the user
- The current search is keyword based supporting product name, category & key features
- The product list is generically filtered based on keywords only, there is no personalisation to include user's taste & preferences
- The reviews are not searchable or filterable (e.g. recent, negative) in order to use them while making buying decision
- Checkout is a multi-step process which adds to the overall slow buying journey

Proposed solution:
The search needs to improve so that the user can find the right product quickly, by providing all the requirements in natural language (including product features, specifics, reviews/ratings, delivery timelines, seller ratings, brand preference) and getting a finite list of highly personalised products (based on user's purchase history of similar products or general preferences such as price range, design, style, prefered brand etc.)

With LLM based search (GenAI-powered discovery), the product criteria can be described in natural language such as expected material, colour, dimensions, delivery timelines, preferred no of product choices (top 5, 10) etc. This combined with personalised product filtering for user (based on price range, brand & other preferences) will result in most relevant search results that will have high conversion rate. This will drastically reduce the shopping time, overall building trust & reliance on the platform.
